<p><p>Ceramic composite membranes are used in various industries for the separation, filtration, and purification of liquids and gases. The market for these membranes can be categorized into three types: flat-sheet membrane, pipe membrane, and other markets. The flat-sheet membrane is a thin, flat structure widely used for ultrafiltration and microfiltration applications in wastewater treatment and drinking water purification. Pipe membranes, on the other hand, are cylindrical in shape and are utilized in applications such as water desalination and gas separation. The "other" market includes niche applications like medical devices, biotechnology, and food processing, where ceramic composite membranes find specific use.</p></p>
