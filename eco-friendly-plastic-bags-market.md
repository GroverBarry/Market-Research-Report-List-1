<p><p>The eco-friendly plastic bags market consists of three types: Polylactic acid (PLA), Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), and Starch Blends. PLA bags are made from renewable resources like corn starch, offering better carbon footprint and biodegradability. PHA bags are produced using bacteria that consume organic materials and convert them into biodegradable polymers. Starch blend bags are made from a mixture of natural starch and petroleum-based polymers, making them both compostable and sustainable. These eco-friendly options aim to reduce the environmental impact of traditional plastic bags by offering biodegradability and sustainability.</p></p>
