<p><p>Oriented cold rolled electrical steel is categorized into three main types: conventional, high magnetic strength, and domain refinement. Conventional steel is commonly used in electrical transformers and motors, providing low core losses. High magnetic strength steel features increased magnetic induction, making it suitable for applications requiring higher efficiency, such as generators and hybrid vehicles. Domain refinement steel undergoes special processing to reduce core losses even further, making it ideal for high-performance electrical devices like electric vehicles and wind turbines. These different types cater to varying needs in the electrical steel market.</p></p>
