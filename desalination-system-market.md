<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The desalination system market is divided into two main types: thermal desalination processes and membrane desalination processes. Thermal desalination processes include multi-stage flash distillation (MSF), multiple effect distillation (MED), and vapor compression distillation (VCD). These processes use heat to separate seawater into fresh water and salt. On the other hand, membrane desalination processes involve the use of membranes to separate salt and impurities from the water. This includes electrodialysis (ED)/electrodialysis reversal (EDR) and reverse osmosis (RO). Another method called ion exchange is also used to remove charged ions from water.</p></p>
