<p><p>The Fingerprint Sensor Market has three main types: Capacitive, Optical, and Thermal sensors. Capacitive sensors use electrical current to detect and map the ridges and valleys of a fingerprint. They are widely used due to their high accuracy and fast response time. Optical sensors capture an image of the fingerprint and analyze the light reflected from it. They are less expensive but can be affected by dirt or moisture. Thermal sensors detect the variations in fingerprint temperature and are less affected by external factors, making them suitable for rugged environments.</p></p>
