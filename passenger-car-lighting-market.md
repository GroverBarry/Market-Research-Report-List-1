Xenon lights utilize xenon gas to produce a bright and intense light that closely resembles natural daylight. Halogen lights use a tungsten filament surrounded by a halogen gas that enhances the light intensity and lifespan. LED lights utilize light-emitting diodes to produce bright and energy-efficient lighting. These lighting technologies are widely used in passenger cars to provide illumination for headlights, taillights, turn signals, and interior lighting.</p></p>
