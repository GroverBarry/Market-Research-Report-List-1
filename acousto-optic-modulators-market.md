<p><p>Acousto-Optic Modulators (AOM) are devices used to manipulate laser beams by varying their intensity, frequency, or phase. The market for AOM can be divided into two types: Fiber-Coupled Acousto-Optic Modulators and Free-Space Acousto-Optic Modulators. </p><p>Fiber-Coupled AOMs utilize optical fibers to transmit laser beams, allowing for easy integration into existing optical systems. They are preferred in applications requiring compactness and stability, such as telecommunications and laser-based instrumentation.</p><p>Free-Space AOMs, on the other hand, operate without the use of fibers and are suitable for applications where high power handling and large beam sizes are required, such as materials processing and laser printing. They provide greater flexibility but may have a larger footprint.</p></p>
