<p><p>Electro-Diagnostic Devices market types include various diagnostic tools used to measure the electrical activities in the body. Electromyogram (EMG) devices are used to assess muscle health and identify nerve damage. Nerve Conduction (NCS) devices measure the speed and intensity of electrical signals in the nerves. Electrocardiography (ECG) devices monitor heart activity and detect abnormalities. Electroencephalography (EEG) devices record brain wave patterns to diagnose neurological conditions. Electroretinography (ERG) devices assess retinal functions. Electrogastrography (EGG) devices measure stomach movement and diagnose gastrointestinal disorders. Other devices serve different specific purposes within this market.</p></p>
