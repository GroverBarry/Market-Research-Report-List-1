<p><p>Particle board, also known as chipboard, is a type of engineered wood product commonly used in the construction industry. It is made by compressing wood chips, sawmill shavings, and other wood residues together with a resin binder. The resulting product is a dense and durable board that is affordable and versatile. </p><p>Particle board for construction has gained popularity due to its various advantages. It is lightweight, making it easy to handle and transport. It also has excellent dimensional stability and is resistant to warping and shrinking, making it suitable for applications such as flooring, roofing, and wall paneling. Additionally, particle board is eco-friendly as it utilizes wood waste and requires less energy to produce compared to solid wood products.</p><p>The future of the particle board for construction market looks promising. With the increasing demand for affordable housing and sustainable building materials, particle board is expected to witness significant growth.
