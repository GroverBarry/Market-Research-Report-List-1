<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The 1,4-Butanediol market comprises four main types: Reppe process, Davy process, Butadiene process, and Propylene oxide process. The Reppe process involves the reaction of acetylene and formaldehyde to produce 1,4-butanediol. The Davy process is based on the reaction between maleic anhydride and H2. The Butadiene process combines butadiene and formaldehyde to create 1,4-butanediol. Lastly, the Propylene oxide process involves the reaction between propylene oxide and formaldehyde. These processes play a significant role in the production and supply of 1,4-butanediol in the market.</p></p>
