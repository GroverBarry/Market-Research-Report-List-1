<p><p>The Automotive Occupant Classification System (OCS) market can be categorized into four types. Visual-based OCS utilizes cameras to detect the presence and position of occupants in a vehicle. Pressure-based OCS uses pressure sensors within seats to determine if a seat is occupied and the weight of the occupant. Weight-based OCS measures the weight of the occupant using load cells embedded in the seat. Electric Field-based OCS employs electric fields to sense the presence and position of occupants.</p></p>
