Actuators are mechanisms that convert energy into mechanical motion to control the valves. Modules consist of multiple valves and actuators integrated into a single unit, enabling centralized control and monitoring. These components play a crucial role in various industries, providing automated and efficient control over pneumatic systems for enhanced productivity and operational efficiency.</p></p>
